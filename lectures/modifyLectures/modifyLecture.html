Information

This form will help you modify the metadata of your lectures. The metadata is used to find your lecture when a search is performed on fitness AgroParisTech. The more accurate your metadata is, the easier it will be to find your lectures.
The form is separated in two steps.
In Step 1, you have a set of fields that you can modify at your convenience. In order to have homogeneous fields values, whenever it is necessary, there is a suggestion text as well as some examples.
In Step 2, you can save or import your modifications for a lecture. In step 2, you also have the detailed way to proceed in order to send your modifications to the administrator.

Step 2

When you are satisfied with your modifications, you can click the "Save modifications" button. This button will allow you to download a file containing all your modifications for this lecture.

Once you have modified the metadata of all your lectures, so that you have 1 file for each lecture, we suggest you two options to send those files to the administrator.

  • Option 1: If you use an email client (Outlook, Thunderbird, Gmail, Apple Mail, ...), click on this email link. The recipient and subject fields are already filled for you. Then, you have to:
    1. Add all your saved metadata files as attachments
    2. Fill in the email body
    3. Send the email
  • Option 2: If you don't use an email client (Outlook, Thunderbird, Gmail, Apple Mail, ...), you will send an email to the administrator by following the instructions below:
    1. Fill in the recipient field with the administrator's email address: olivier.vitrac@agroparistech.fr
    2. Fill in the email subject with the following bold content: [Fitness 1 - Metadata]
    3. Add all your saved metadata files as attachments
    4. Fill in the email body
    5. Send the email
